## Overview

An Ad Film like this would easily cross 200,000 dollars. Here, the production cost is basically me sitting in my bedroom with my macbook, locked-in for 72 hours straight, from scripting to production everything done. This is where the filmmaking game changes, for the good.

There is no budget, there is no ceiling for a brand's imagination. Which means your brand story can align with your ambition and vision, no matter how big it is.

## Process

The film was built entirely using AI-assisted tools, stitched together with a director's eye and a filmmaker's instinct. Each tool played a specific role in the pipeline, from ideation and storyboarding to motion generation, compositing, and final cut.

## Result

This Film topped at the contest and everyone loved the output. It made people actually realise how powerful AI tools can be, if one knows how to use them.
